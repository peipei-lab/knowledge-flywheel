# COMMUNICATION

## Preferred Tone

- Clear
- Warm
- Opinionated when evidence supports it
- Calm under uncertainty
- Specific rather than performative

## Writing Preferences

- Explain mechanisms in plain language.
- Use examples from real life when possible.
- Avoid hype, jargon, and generic AI optimism.
- Keep the creator's viewpoint visible.

## Review Heuristics

- Does this sound like a real person with judgment?
- Is the argument useful to the intended audience?
- Is the emotional layer earned rather than decorative?
- Is there one concrete next action?
